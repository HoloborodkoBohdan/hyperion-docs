## System Users

### FOP Manager
**Primary administrator** of the FOP management system with full access to all functionality.

**Responsibilities:**
- Manage FOP employee records and registration data
- Create and maintain FOP contracts and templates
- Configure contract types
- Monitor KVED compliance
- Oversee billing and payment processes

**Security Group:** `ua_fop.group_fop_manager`

### Account Manager
**Responsible manager** assigned to specific FOP employees.

**Responsibilities:**
- Manage assigned FOP employees
- Process batch payments and validate amounts
- Post FOP bills after verification
- Import and manage bank statements
- Coordinate with FOPs on contract and payment matters

**Access Level:** Accounting module access + FOP billing limited to assigned employees

**Security Group:** `ua_fop.group_fop_user`
**Field Reference:** `hr.employee.responsible_account_manager_id`

### HR Manager
**Human resources specialist** managing employee data.

**Responsibilities:**
- Create and maintain employee records
- Manage HR contracts and set contract types
- Maintain employee personal data

**Access Level:** HR module access + basic FOP employee data
**Related Models:** `hr.employee`, `hr.contract`

## Business Entities

### FOP Employee
**Ukrainian individual entrepreneur** (Фізична Особа-Підприємець) registered as a contractor providing services to the company.

**Characteristics:**
- Has Ukrainian tax registration
- Assigned KVED business activity codes
- May be in 3rd tax group
- Receives payments for services rendered

**Related Model:** `hr.employee` (with `is_fop=True`)
